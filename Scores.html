Originally intended as a prelude to my Brass Quintet, the following is my initial sketch:

"Source" marks what I would consider my first successful attempt at applying formal structure to a composition and is the first movement of three in a brass quintet written for my graduation recital at the University of Lethbridge.

Inspired by Seth Godin's book of the same title, "The Dip" is the first chamber piece I have fully notated and is the second movement of three in a brass quintet written for my graduation recital at the University of Lethbridge.

“Exit Strategy” is the final movement of three in a brass quintet written for my graduation recital at the University of Lethbridge.
