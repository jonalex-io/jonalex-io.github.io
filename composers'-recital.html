Event Horizon Composer's Collective Presents: Sonic Innovations

SAT, MAR 2, 2024 7:30 PM TO 10:30 PM

This event is from the archives of The Notice Board. The event has already taken place and the information contained in this post may no longer be relevant or accurate.

Mark your calendars for March 2, 7:30pm and join us in the Recital Hall for an evening of transcendence through sound as the Event Horizon Composer's Collective proudly presents Sonic Innovations. This annual student composer's concert will showcase the extraordinary talents of burgeoning and emerging music composers.

Department of Music composition students, under the direction of Dr. Arlan Schultz will present an eclectic range of new works. Experience compositions by students Nic Sherman, Luke Creed, Charlie Peach, Morgan Brooks, Cori Campbell, Jonathan Alexander, Brenna Le May, and the Spontaneous Combustion Ensemble.

This innovative concert is sure to be an auditory feast, offering a rich tapestry of compositions that push the boundaries of conventional sound.

Admission is free and all are welcome!

No tickets needed. Admission is free for all.
