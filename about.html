Jonathan Alexander is a Canadian composer, multi-instrumentalist producer, and classically trained jazz musician. Born to Scottish and Taishanese parents, with Mandarin as his second language, Jonathan’s background infuses his music with a unique blend of traditional orchestration, cross-cultural composition, and modern sound design, creating immersive aural landscapes that enhance storytelling across various media, from film to games and the metaverse.

Jonathan’s musical journey began at age five with classical piano training under the Royal Conservatory of Music. While excelling in his early years, his growing fascination with jazz improvisation, inspired by Hiromi Uehara, Oscar Peterson, and Chick Corea, led him beyond the classical canon. This pivot ignited a passion for musical exploration that extended far beyond Western traditions. Jonathan’s curiosity drove him to explore a diverse array of instruments, from the rhythmic intricacies of flamenco guitar to the haunting melodies of the Chinese erhu. His journey into multi-instrumentalism naturally evolved to embrace modern digital audio production tools, further expanding his musical palette.

Jonathan’s compositional career took flight at UBC Vancouver’s game development club, where he scored his first VR game and, as part of a collaborative team, earned several accolades, including the year-end showcase winner and the GameTech Platinum Award. This experience inspired him to pursue formal studies in music composition at the University of Lethbridge. Under Dr. Arlan N. Schultz’s mentorship, Jonathan honed his skills in concert music while continuing to compose for games and animations, collaborating on multiple films with students from Sheridan College’s prestigious animation program. Throughout his studies, he immersed himself in the entire range of orchestral instruments, further developing his multi-instrumentalist abilities and deepening his understanding of orchestration.

Jonathan’s graduation recital in March 2024 showcased his collaborative spirit and diverse musical influences, marking the culmination of his formal studies and the beginning of his freelance career. Since then, his portfolio has expanded to include crafting immersive metaverse soundscapes for Smobler’s 3VEREST, composing emotive scores for indie films, and creating bespoke arrangements for various commissioned works.

蔣智海是一位加拿大作曲家、多樂器製作人和受過古典訓練的爵士音樂家。他出生於蘇格蘭和台山父母，以普通話為第二語言，智海的背景為他的音樂注入了獨特的傳統配器、跨文化作曲和現代音效設計的融合，創造出沉浸式的聽覺景觀，增強了從電影到遊戲和元宇宙等各種媒體的故事敘述。

智海的音樂之旅始於五歲時在皇家音樂學院接受的古典鋼琴訓練。雖然他在早年表現出色，但受到上原廣美、奧斯卡·彼得森和奇克·柯瑞亞的啟發，他對爵士即興演奏的興趣日益濃厚，這使他超越了古典音樂的範疇。這一轉變點燃了他對音樂探索的熱情，遠遠超出了西方傳統。智海的好奇心驅使他探索各種各樣的樂器，從佛朗明哥吉他的節奏複雜性到中國二胡的淒美旋律。他進入多樂器演奏的旅程自然而然地發展到現代數字音頻製作工具，進一步擴展了他的音樂調色板。

智海的作曲事業始於UBC溫哥華分校的遊戲開發俱樂部，在那裡他為第一個VR遊戲譜曲，並作為合作團隊的一員贏得了多項榮譽，包括年終展示冠軍和GameTech白金獎。這次經歷激發了他在萊斯布里奇大學正式學習音樂作曲的決心。在Arlan N. Schultz博士的指導下，智海磨練了他的音樂會音樂技巧，同時繼續為遊戲和動畫作曲，與謝爾丹學院著名動畫專業的學生合作製作了多部電影。在學習期間，他沉浸在整個範圍的管弦樂器中，進一步發展了他的多樂器演奏能力，並加深了對配器的理解。

智海於2024年3月的畢業音樂會展示了他的合作精神和多元化的音樂影響，標誌著他正式學習的結束和自由職業生涯的開始。從那時起，他的作品組合已擴展到為Smobler的3VEREST創作沉浸式元宇宙音景、為獨立電影譜寫富有情感的配樂，以及為各種委託作品創作定制安排。
