Jonathan Alexander is a Canadian composer and multi-instrumentalist producer whose music blends cross-cultural orchestration with cutting-edge sound design. Selected for the esteemed SOCAN Foundation 2024 Screen Music Lab at Ubisoft Montreal, Jonathan’s portfolio spans immersive soundscapes for The Sandbox Metaverse, emotive piano scores for indie films, and commissioned orchestral arrangements.

Born to Scottish and Taishanese parents, with Mandarin as his second language, Jonathan’s multicultural heritage infuses his ability to create immersive aural landscapes that enhance storytelling across various media. His musical journey began with classical piano at age five under the Royal Conservatory of Music, later expanding into jazz improvisation inspired by virtuosos like Hiromi Uehara and Oscar Peterson. This eclectic foundation sparked a passion for multi-instrumentalism, encompassing everything from flamenco guitar to Chinese erhu. His passion for exploration extended into digital audio production, where he innovates by blending acoustic textures with modern, often non-linear, sound design.

While studying music composition at the University of Lethbridge under Dr. Arlan N. Schultz, Jonathan honed his skills in orchestration and concert music while building a diverse portfolio of media compositions. His early career highlights include scoring a VR game with UBC Vancouver’s game development club—leading to accolades such as the 2021 GameTech Platinum Award—and collaborating on animated films with Sheridan College students. These experiences solidified his reputation as a versatile composer across mediums.

Since concluding his studies in 2024, Jonathan has expanded his portfolio with projects including Smobler’s 3VEREST Project alongside commissioned film and orchestral works. His artistry reflects a deep commitment to storytelling through music, while thriving in collaborative partnerships—“The most compelling musical narratives emerge from the intersection of diverse perspectives and talents.”

蔣智海是一位加拿大作曲家和多樂器製作人，其音樂融合了跨文化的管弦樂編曲和尖端的聲音設計。智海入選了備受推崇的SOCAN基金會2024年在育碧蒙特利爾舉辦的螢幕音樂實驗室，他的作品涵蓋了The Sandbox元宇宙的沉浸式音景、獨立電影的情感鋼琴配樂，以及委託的管弦樂編曲。

智海出生於一個蘇格蘭和台山混血的家庭，普通話是他的第二語言。這種多元文化背景賦予了他創造沉浸式聽覺景觀的能力，能夠增強各種媒體中的故事講述。他的音樂之旅始於五歲時在皇家音樂學院學習古典鋼琴，後來受到上原廣美和奧斯卡·彼得森等大師的啟發，擴展到爵士即興演奏。這種多元化的基礎激發了他對多樂器演奏的熱情，涵蓋了從佛朗明哥吉他到中國二胡等各種樂器。他對探索的熱情延伸到數字音頻製作領域，在那裡他通過將原聲紋理與現代、常常是非線性的聲音設計相結合來進行創新。

在萊斯布里奇大學跟隨Arlan N. Schultz博士學習音樂作曲期間，智海磨練了他的配器和音樂會音樂技能，同時建立了多樣化的媒體作曲作品集。他早期職業生涯的亮點包括為溫哥華不列顛哥倫比亞大學遊戲開發俱樂部的VR遊戲配樂——這讓他獲得了2021年GameTech白金獎等榮譽——以及與謝爾丹學院的學生合作製作動畫電影。這些經歷鞏固了他作為跨媒體多才多藝作曲家的聲譽。

自2024年完成學業以來，智海擴展了他的作品集，包括與Smobler的3VEREST項目合作，以及受委託的電影和管弦樂作品。他的藝術創作反映了他對通過音樂講述故事的深刻承諾，同時在協作夥伴關係中蓬勃發展——“最引人入勝的音樂敘事源於多元化視角和才能的交匯處。”
